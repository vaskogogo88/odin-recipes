Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish consisting of a flattened, leavened bread dough baked at a high temperature with toppings such as tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients. It is a popular and versatile food, often cooked in a wood-fired oven, and can be garnished with a wide variety of items, from pepperoni to vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozarella
